1. Chrome & Mirror Finishes

01

Liquid Metal Nails

Chrome and mirror-finish nails are dominating 2026. The silver and rose-gold chrome look is especially popular — it catches light beautifully in the summer sun and pairs perfectly with any outfit. Our clients obsess over this look for weddings and events.

Available at T-T Nails & Spa

Chrome nails work best on coffin or almond shapes and pair beautifully with our Builder Gel or Gel X extensions. The metallic pigment is applied over cured gel for a long-lasting, high-shine result that won't chip like regular polish.

3. Ombré & Baby Boomer

03

The baby boomer — a soft gradient from blush pink to white — never goes out of style. In summer 2026, it's evolved with subtle color twists: peach-to-white, lavender-to-clear, and even coral-to-champagne ombré effects are trending hard.

This style is incredibly versatile — elegant enough for formal occasions, casual enough for everyday wear. It's also one of the longest-lasting styles we offer, especially done in Builder Gel or Acrylic, making it a fantastic investment for your summer nails.

How to Make Your Summer Nails Last Longer

Getting beautiful nails is one thing — keeping them looking great all summer is another. Here are our top tips from the T-T Nails & Spa team:

  • Wear gloves when cleaning — household chemicals are the #1 enemy of gel and acrylic nails
  • Moisturize your cuticles daily — well-hydrated cuticles prevent lifting at the base
  • Book fills every 2–3 weeks — don't wait until nails are damaged; a timely fill keeps your set looking fresh
  • Avoid using nails as tools — opening cans or scratching off stickers causes breakage
  • Apply cuticle oil nightly — this extends the life of your polish and keeps nails flexible
  • Stay out of chlorine when possible — pool water can weaken gel bonds over time
