What is the best nail extension product in 2026? +
One of the best nail extension options in 2026 is Builder Gel, also known as Structure Gel. It is strong, flexible, lightweight, and designed for long-lasting wear.
What nail services do you offer in Warrensburg, MO? +
We offer Gel X extensions, acrylic nails, builder gel, dip powder, manicures, spa pedicures, and custom nail art — all using premium products.
Do you accept walk-ins? +

How do I book an appointment? +
Simply call us at (660) 747-2247 to schedule a time that works for you. We'll confirm your service and get you in as soon as possible.
What is Gel X and how is it different from acrylic? +
Gel X uses pre-shaped soft gel tips bonded with gel polish — no monomer, no drilling. They're lighter, more flexible, and gentler on your natural nails than traditional acrylics.
How long do nail extensions last? +
With proper care, Gel X and acrylic extensions typically last 3–4 weeks. We recommend fill appointments every 2–3 weeks to keep your nails looking fresh.
