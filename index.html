What is Gel X and how is it different from acrylic? +
Gel X uses pre-shaped soft gel tips bonded with gel polish — no monomer, no drilling. They're lighter, more flexible, and gentler on your natural nails than traditional acrylics.
How long do nail extensions last? +
With proper care, Gel X and acrylic extensions typically last 3–4 weeks. We recommend fill appointments every 2–3 weeks to keep your nails looking fresh.
